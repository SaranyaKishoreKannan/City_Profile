About The City

It is an Indian district in the north-east of Kerala state with administrative headquarters at the municipality of Kalpetta. It is set high in the Western Ghats with altitudes ranging from 700 to 2100 meters.

The city has an area of 2,132 km sq km.Wayanad is the only district in Kerala that shares its borders with Karnataka and Tamil Nadu. For this reason, it is often visited by tourists from these states.
