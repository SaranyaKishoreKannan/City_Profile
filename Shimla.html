About:

Shimla is the capital of Himachal Pradesh and a popular hill-station.The average rainfall of District Shimla is 1028 mm. The district has an area of 5,131 sq. km out of a total area of 55,673 sq. km. of Himachal Pradesh according to the Surveyor General of India.
